About

Pakistan Open Data will be available on 14th August 2017, over 100 data sets will be available on platform catalog.

Open Data Portal Pakistan is an initiative to get your organizational or generic data available publicly. ODPP enables you to piblish your data sets on single platform which can be utilize by various number of peaople and organizations
